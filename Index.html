Who We Are

Dedicated to Getting the Job Done Properly

Full Rewires

Complete rewiring of residential and commercial properties. We manage everything from stripping the old installation to final certification — minimising disruption throughout.

Home Refurbishments

Electrical work for full home renovations, extensions, and loft conversions. We work closely with builders and project managers to keep your build on schedule.
